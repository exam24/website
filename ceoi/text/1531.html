CSES - Information

This site contains a collection of Central European Olympiad in Informatics contests. At the moment the contests from 2005 to 2021 are available; new contests may be added in the future.

There are two ways to practice: you can either take a virtual contest where you have five hours time to solve the problems (as in the original contest), or then you can upsolve the problems without limitations.

The contests have their original tests and subtasks (if specified in the problem statement). The time limits have been adjusted for the CSES contest system. All contests have full feedback.

If you have found errors or have other feedback, please send a message to ahslaaks@cs.helsinki.fi (Antti Laaksonen).
